Tacos

These are the best tacos

Description of the best taco recipe and how it will change your life

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Steak
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Sour Cream
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat up the tortilla on the stove
  2. Put the meat inside
  3. Add cheese first so it melts
  4. Sprinkle on diced onions
  5. Add cilantro
  6. Add a dab or sour cream
  7. Top off with hot sauce
  8. Eat!
